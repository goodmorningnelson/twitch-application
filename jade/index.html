Kapsuli

Founded in France with two friends to help the streaming community, Kapsuli is a social media platform designed to help Twitch broadcasters to build a strong social presence.

Kapsuli features a way to embed a live Twitch stream visible within Twitter. It also allows broadcasters to program tweets to be sent automatically while on the air, transforming the way broadcasters interact with their followers, as well as helping them grow a larger audience.

The Kapsuli Player

• Easy and fast way to embed your live feed into Twitter.

• A customizable player, helping you build your brand.

• Real estate for additional revenue stream through extended sponsor exposure.
